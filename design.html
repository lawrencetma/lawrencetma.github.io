Importance of Web Design



Web design can make or break a brand or business and it is extremely important that both be strong and convey the correct message to users and customers.

When designing a website I try to keep in mind that we are trying to promote a brand, a message and an image to the viewer that will give them a lasting impression of you or your company. So everything from the clean design feel, to the actual functionality of the site should reflect what you or your business is all about.

Good website design will let your users navigate to the correct pages when they want as well as let them do it quickly and easily. There will be consistency of style and brand so users are never confused as to what you do and why you have a web presence. Most importantly, good website design builds trust in your customers that your business is serious about providing them with the best quality in all areas including giving a great first impression with your website's impeccable design.
