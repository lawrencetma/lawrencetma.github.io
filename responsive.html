Responsive Design



So what exactly is responsive web design? Responsive web design is the principle that one website can be made to look great on any device or computer regardless of screen size. It gives your viewers or customers one stop that they can go to and the design adapts to the device or computer that they are using at the time.

In a world that is being dominated by smartphones and tablets, people are accessing the internet in a multitude of different ways and businesses must adapt to those gateways.

Whenever possible I try to incorporate responsive web design into my work so that users and viewers are able to visit and enjoy the site no matter how they choose to do so. If I'm able to give all different screen sizes a customized and clean feel then I feel that it allows your site the flexibility to work for you in all areas.
