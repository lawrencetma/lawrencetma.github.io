The Bay Area Burger Battle

What Happens when the Best Burger Places in the Bay Area go bun to bun?

The San Francisco Bay Area is home to some of the best food in the world, so why wouldn't it also be home to some of the best burgers money can buy?
We tried a burger from each of the most famous and popular burger joints around the Bay Area to give you the heads up on the best burger around.

  • In-N-Out Burger
  • Five Guys Burgers and Fries
  • Roam Artisan Burger
  • Umami Burger
  • Super Duper Burger

Big Mouth Burgers
