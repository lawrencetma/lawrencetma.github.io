How to make it at home
  1. cook 3 cups of sushi rice in a standard rice cooker
  2. use 10"x10" seaweed wrappers and place them on the counter
  3. place musubi frame along the center line of a piece of seaweed wrap
  4. add one layer of rice
  5. add one layer of pan fried spam
  6. add seasoning and another layer of rice
  7. remove musubi frame and wrap seaweed around the rice
  8. cut roll into two pieces, serve and enjoy!
