Why I'm Here

Sometimes you really never know where life will take you. For me, I thought I knew exactly what I wanted to do: Go to law school, pass the bar, be a public interest attorney and save the world. After 3 years of practicing I realized that being an attorney was not what I had envisioned at all and I was ready to make a huge change.

What I realized about myself is that I want to create things, have concrete results based work and be innovative and different. None of these things were happening in my law career, but all of them can be achieved in web development.

About Me

I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area and I absolutely love it here. I'm a huge San Francisco Giants fan and live and die on every game/pitch/inning.

I went to college at the University of Arizona before I attended law school at the University of the Pacific McGeorge School of Law. I'm currently working at an apprenticeship at Deploy and recently graduated from DevBootcamp, an intensive 19 week program focused on full stack programming. I currently live in San Bruno, California not too far from my family and beloved Giants.
